Cards

Dashboards are composed of cards.

cards are the basic building blocks of dashboards you often won’t need to manually define a card – they will typically map to cells in your notebook that create plots, display data, display text, etc.

Rows and columns

Cards are arranged into rows and columns.

to organize content arrange into rows and columns

Layouts

Pages, tabsets, and sidebars allow for more advanced layouts.

can also control the general layout of the page with things like pages, tabsets, and sidebars talk about this in the next slide deck
